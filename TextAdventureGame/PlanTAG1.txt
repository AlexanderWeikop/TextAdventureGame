Basic game, maze of rooms, player can move around in maze, each room has text description, each room has up to 4 entrances to other rooms

Starting room is RoomA2, exit is RoomC5, grid is A1-F5
Win condition is reaching the exit (RoomC5)



Setting: Medieval-ish



Story: Player starts without a torch and must find their way to the exit, to get there, the player must pass through a series of rooms

which might hide danger or other surprises.
